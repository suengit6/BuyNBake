Kid-Friendly Treats

Colorful Fruit Kabobs


Ingredients:

  • Assorted fresh fruits (strawberries, grapes, pineapple chunks, melon balls, etc.)
  • Wooden skewers

Instructions:

  1. Wash and prepare the fruits. Cut larger fruits into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Thread the fruit pieces onto the wooden skewers in a colorful pattern.
  3. Arrange the fruit kabobs on a serving platter and enjoy a healthy and delicious treat!

Funfetti Cupcakes


Ingredients:

  • 1 box vanilla cake mix
  • 1 cup rainbow sprinkles
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Vanilla frosting
  • Additional sprinkles for decoration

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a cupcake pan with paper liners.
  2. In a large bowl, mix together cake mix, rainbow sprinkles, milk, melted butter, eggs, and vanilla extract until well combined.
  3. Spoon the batter into the cupcake liners and bake for 18-20 minutes or until a toothpick inserted comes out clean.
  4. Cool the cupcakes completely before frosting with vanilla frosting and decorating with additional sprinkles.

Teddy Bear Toasts


Ingredients:

  • Whole wheat bread slices
  • Peanut butter
  • Banana slices
  • Raisins

Instructions:

  1. Toast the whole wheat bread slices until golden brown.
  2. Spread peanut butter on each toast slice.
  3. Place banana slices on top as ears and use raisins to create eyes and a nose.
  4. Create a cute teddy bear face on each toast and serve for a delightful and nutritious snack.
