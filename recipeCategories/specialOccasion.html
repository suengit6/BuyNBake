Elegant Red Velvet Cake


Ingredients:

  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3 tablespoons cocoa powder
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups vegetable oil
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 tablespoons red food coloring
  • 1 teaspoon white vinegar

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour three 9-inch round cake pans.
  2. In a large bowl, sift together flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, salt, and cocoa powder.
  3. In a separate bowl, beat eggs, then add vegetable oil, buttermilk, vanilla extract, red food coloring, and vinegar. Mix well.
  4. Combine wet and dry ingredients until smooth. Divide the batter evenly into the prepared pans.
  5. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  6. Cool the cakes in the pans for 10 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely before frosting.

Decadent Black Forest Cake


Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans.
  2. In a large bowl, sift together flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla extract. Beat until well combined.
  4. Stir in boiling water until the batter is smooth. The batter will be thin, but that's okay.
  5. Pour the batter evenly into the prepared pans and bake for 30 to 35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  6. Cool the cakes in the pans for 10 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely before frosting.

Celebration Rainbow Cake


Ingredients:

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon pure vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups whole milk
  • Gel food coloring (assorted colors)
  • 4 cups whipped cream or buttercream frosting
  • Rainbow sprinkles for decoration

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour three 8-inch round cake pans.
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together flour, baking powder, and salt.
  3. In a large bowl, cream together butter and sugar until light and fluffy. Add eggs one at a time, beating well with each addition. Stir in vanilla extract.
  4. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, alternating with the milk. Begin and end with the dry ingredients.
  5. Divide the batter into separate bowls and tint each with different colors of gel food coloring.
  6. Drop spoonfuls of colored batter into the prepared pans, creating a rainbow effect.
  7. Bake for 25 to 30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  8. Cool the cakes in the pans for 10 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely before frosting.
