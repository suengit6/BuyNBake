Classic French Baguette


Ingredients:

  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 tablespoon active dry yeast
  • 1 ½ teaspoons salt
  • 1 ½ cups warm water
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Cornmeal (for dusting)

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let it sit for 5 minutes until foamy.
  2. In a large bowl, mix flour and salt. Add the yeast mixture and olive oil. Knead until you have a smooth dough.
  3. Cover the dough and let it rise until doubled in size (about 1 hour).
  4. Punch down the dough and divide it into two portions. Shape each into a baguette and place on a baking sheet dusted with cornmeal.
  5. Let the baguettes rise again for 30 minutes. Preheat the oven to 450°F (230°C).
  6. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until golden brown. Cool before slicing.

Blueberry Muffins


Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 ½ cups fresh or frozen blueberries

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). Line a muffin tin with paper liners.
  2. In a bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In another bowl, mix buttermilk, melted butter, and eggs. Add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients, stirring until just combined.
  4. Gently fold in the blueberries. Spoon the batter into the muffin cups.
  5. Bake for 18-20 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  6. Cool in the tin for 5 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.

Cinnamon Rolls


Ingredients:

  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/3 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 packet active dry yeast
  • 1 cup warm milk
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 large egg
  • For the Filling:
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons ground cinnamon

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine warm milk, sugar, and yeast. Let it sit for 5 minutes until foamy.
  2. In a large bowl, mix flour and salt. Add the yeast mixture, melted butter, and egg. Knead until the dough is smooth.
  3. Cover the dough and let it rise until doubled in size (about 1 hour).
  4. Roll out the dough on a floured surface into a rectangle. Spread softened butter over the surface and sprinkle with brown sugar and cinnamon.
  5. Roll up the dough and cut into slices. Place the slices in a greased baking dish.
  6. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 25-30 minutes or until golden brown. Drizzle with icing if desired.
