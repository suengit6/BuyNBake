"Banana Oatmeal Muffins"

These muffins are a healthier option, incorporating bananas and oats for added nutrition. They're perfect for those looking for a delicious yet wholesome treat.

Ingredients:

  • 2 1/4 teaspoons (1 packet) active dry yeast
  • 1/4 cup warm water
  • 1 cup cold milk
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 3 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, cold

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, combine the warm water and yeast. Let it sit for 5 minutes until foamy.
  2. In a separate bowl, heat the cold milk until lukewarm. Add sugar and stir until dissolved.
  3. Combine the yeast mixture and milk mixture.
  4. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour and salt.
  5. Add the wet ingredients to the flour mixture and stir until a dough forms.
  6. Knead the dough on a floured surface until smooth. Wrap in plastic wrap and refrigerate for 1 hour.
  7. Place the cold butter between two sheets of parchment paper and roll it into a rectangle.
  8. Chill the butter block in the refrigerator for 30 minutes.
  9. Roll out the chilled dough on a floured surface into a larger rectangle.
  10. Place the chilled butter block on one half of the dough and fold the other half over it.
  11. Roll out the dough again and fold it into thirds. Repeat this process two more times.
  12. Roll out the dough into a large rectangle and cut it into triangles.
  13. Roll each triangle into a croissant shape and place them on a baking sheet.
  14. Let the croissants rise for 1-2 hours until doubled in size.
  15. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  16. Brush the croissants with an egg wash and bake for 15-20 minutes or until golden brown.
  17. Cool on a wire rack before serving.
