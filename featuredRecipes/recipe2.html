"Banana Oatmeal Muffins"

These muffins are a healthier option, incorporating bananas and oats for added nutrition. They're perfect for those looking for a delicious yet wholesome treat.

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1/3 cup melted butter
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • Pinch of salt
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1 large egg, beaten
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup rolled oats

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease or line a muffin tin with paper liners.
  2. In a mixing bowl, mash the ripe bananas with a fork.
  3. Melt the butter and add it to the mashed bananas. Stir well.
  4. Add the baking soda and a pinch of salt. Mix thoroughly.
  5. Stir in the sugar, beaten egg, and vanilla extract until well combined.
  6. Gradually add the flour, mixing just until incorporated.
  7. Fold in the rolled oats, ensuring they are evenly distributed in the batter.
  8. Spoon the batter into the muffin cups, filling each about two-thirds full.
  9. Bake in the preheated oven for 18-20 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  10. Allow the muffins to cool in the tin for 5 minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.
