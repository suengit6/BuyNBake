Baking Recipes

Cake Classics

  • Rich Chocolate Cake

    Indulge in the decadence of this classic chocolate cake.

    Ingredients:

    • 2 cups all-purpose flour
    • 1¾ cups granulated sugar
    • ¾ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
    • 1½ teaspoons baking powder
    • 1½ teaspoons baking soda
    • 1 teaspoon salt
    • 2 large eggs
    • 1 cup whole milk
    • ½ cup vegetable oil
    • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
    • 1 cup boiling water

    Steps:

    1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
    2. Sift together flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
    3. Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla extract; beat until smooth.
    4. Stir in boiling water until well combined.
    5. Pour the batter into a greased and floured baking pan.
    6. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until a toothpick inserted comes out clean.
    7. Cool completely before frosting.

Healthy Bakes

  • Low-Sugar Blueberry Muffins

    Delicious muffins with reduced sugar content and bursts of blueberry flavor.

    Ingredients:

    • 1½ cups all-purpose flour
    • ½ cup granulated sugar
    • 2 teaspoons baking powder
    • ½ teaspoon salt
    • ½ cup unsalted butter, melted
    • 2 large eggs
    • ½ cup milk
    • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
    • 1 cup fresh blueberries

    Steps:

    1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
    2. In a large bowl, combine flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
    3. In another bowl, whisk together melted butter, eggs, milk, and vanilla extract.
    4. Add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients and mix until just combined.
    5. Gently fold in the blueberries.
    6. Spoon the batter into muffin cups and bake for 18-20 minutes.
    7. Cool before serving.

Bread and Pastries

  • Homemade Baguettes

    Master the art of creating crispy and delicious baguettes at home.

    Ingredients:

    • 4 cups bread flour
    • 2 teaspoons salt
    • 1½ teaspoons active dry yeast
    • 1¾ cups warm water

    Steps:

    1. In a bowl, combine flour and salt.
    2. In a separate bowl, dissolve yeast in warm water and let it sit for 5 minutes.
    3. Add the yeast mixture to the flour and mix until a dough forms.
    4. Knead the dough on a floured surface for 10 minutes.
    5. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover, and let it rise for 1 hour.
    6. Divide the dough, shape into baguettes, and let them rise for another 30 minutes.
    7. Bake at 450°F (230°C) for 20-25 minutes until golden brown.

Kid-Friendly Treats

  • Funfetti Cupcakes

    Cupcakes filled with colorful sprinkles for a fun and festive treat.

    Ingredients:

    • 1½ cups all-purpose flour
    • 1½ teaspoons baking powder
    • ½ teaspoon salt
    • ½ cup unsalted butter, softened
    • 1 cup granulated sugar
    • 3 large eggs
    • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
    • ½ cup milk
    • ½ cup rainbow sprinkles

    Steps:

    1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
    2. In a bowl, whisk together flour, baking powder, and salt.
    3. In another bowl, cream together butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
    4. Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in vanilla extract.
    5. Gradually add the dry ingredients, alternating with milk. Fold in sprinkles.
    6. Spoon the batter into cupcake liners and bake for 18-20 minutes.
    7. Cool before frosting and decorating.

Special Occasions

  • Red Velvet Celebration Cake

    A show-stopping red velvet cake perfect for birthdays and celebrations.

    Ingredients:

    • 2½ cups all-purpose flour
    • 1½ teaspoons baking powder
    • 1½ teaspoons baking soda
    • 3 tablespoons cocoa powder
    • 1½ cups granulated sugar
    • 1½ cups vegetable oil
    • 3 large eggs
    • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
    • 1 cup buttermilk
    • 2 tablespoons red food coloring
    • 1 teaspoon white vinegar
    • ½ cup brewed coffee, cooled

    Steps:

    1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour cake pans.
    2. In a bowl, sift together flour, baking powder, baking soda, and cocoa powder.
    3. In another bowl, beat together sugar, oil, eggs, and vanilla extract.
    4. Combine buttermilk, red food coloring, vinegar, and coffee in a separate bowl.
    5. Add dry ingredients and buttermilk mixture to the sugar mixture, alternating and ending with dry ingredients.
    6. Divide the batter into cake pans and bake for 25-30 minutes.
    7. Cool completely before frosting.
